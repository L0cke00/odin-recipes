Jollof Rice

Description

This bold-flavored jollof rice, my version of the dish served all over West Africa, will go well with any spicy chicken, meat, or fish.

Ingredients

Blended Sauce:

  • 2 bell peppers, cut into chunks
  • 2 tomatoes, cut into chunks
  • 1 habanero pepper, seeded
  • 1 clove of garlic
  • 1/4 cup of chicken stock

Rice:

  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • 1 small yellow onion, diced
  • 3 tablespoons of tomato paste
  • 1.5 cups of parboiled rice
  • 3 cups of chicken stock
  • 2 tablespoons of curry powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dried thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of ground white pepper
  • 2 bay leaves
  • salt to taste
  • 3.5 tablespoons of salted butter

Instruction

  1. Place peppers, tomatoes, habanero, garlic and 1/4 cup of stock in a blender and blend until smooth. Set this aside.
  2. In a large pot, heat oil over a medium heat. Add diced onion and fry until soft and translucent.
  3. Stir in blended sauce mix, 3 cups of stock, curry powder, thyme, white pepper and bay leaves. Season to taste and stir until it comes to the boil.
  4. Turn the heat down to low, cover the pot and cook until rice has absorbed all the liquid, adding more stock if needed.
  5. Stir in mixed vegetables and butter. Cover and let cook for 5 minutes. Turn heat off and allow the steam to finish cooking vegetables
