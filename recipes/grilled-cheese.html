Grilled Cheese

Description

Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of white bread
  • 3 tablespoons of butter,divided
  • 2 slices of Cheddar cheese

Instruction

  1. Preheat a non stick pan over medium heat. Spread better on one side of the bread. Place bread butter side down in pan and add a slice of cheese. Butter a second slice and place butter side up on the cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side, flip over and continue cooking until cheese has melted. Repeat with the two remaining slices of bread
